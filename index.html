About the Firm

G. Chiranjeevulu & Co. was founded by Late Shri G. Chiranjeevulu, a respected figure in the field of taxation and audit. Today, under the leadership of Managing Director Dr. Gangishetty Pramod Kumar, the firm focuses on accuracy, timely compliance and clear communication. We advise startups, professionals and established businesses on minimizing risk and keeping tax matters in order.
